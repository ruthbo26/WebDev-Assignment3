About GlowCo

GlowCo is an inclusive hair and makeup company founded in 2023, dedicated to celebrating beauty in all its diverse forms. We believe that everyone deserves to feel confident and empowered, which is why our expert team specializes in creating personalized looks for every skin tone, hair texture, and style. Whether you're preparing for a special event or just want to glow up your everyday look, GlowCo is here to help you shine. At GlowCo, beauty has no limits, and neither do we!
